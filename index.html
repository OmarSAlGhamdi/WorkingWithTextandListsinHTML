Welcome to Bethany's Pie Shop

Our history so far

For many years Bethany has been making the most delicious pies at her home in 2013. we opened our first store in Brussels (Belgium) and a cozy little street near the grand market. people from all over the world fell in love with the soft cakes, having cheesecake, spicy apple pies... the store became a success in no time.

In 2015, no less than 100 pies were sold every day and bethany's Body Shop moved into a large building after making a € 2000 revenue. After receiving many requests, it become clear that our next step was making it possible to order pies from the comfort of your own home and from anywhere in the world. You are now looking at the result of this: our very own website, making it possible to order Bethany's delicious pies whenever you feel like it!
